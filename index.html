About us

What is NaviKatz?

NaviKatz is a terrific multichain Token, NFT-Marketplace, and GameFi project built on the Celo, Cronos and Fantom blockchain.

To expand that terrific system, the NaviKatz introduces a unique trio-chain collection of carefully designed NFTs that will support our KatzRatz NFT gaming experience, token and marketplace with several on and off game usecases..

Why A Trio-Chain System?

The idea to run on NaviKatz on Celo, Cronos and Fantom blockchains is premise on the fact these blockchains offer a useful system for storing and accessing tokens.

They support gaming and effectively stand as NFT-enabled blockchains. The TrioChain System also makes NaviKatz an attractive consumer-centric project by giving the Navians with a growing user base and inexpensive transactions.

How can I join them?

1. Free-To-Play: Gamers who doesn’t own an NFT or the Katz token can only engage on the free mode of the game. The free modes gives games a game character with no super ability.

>

2. Stake-To-Play: Gamers can also purchase $Katz token and stake it to get a game character card with limited super abilities.

3. Avatar-To-Play: Gamers who own any NaviKatz Warriors NFT can use it as a game avatar. Ths translates into game characters with super abilities.
